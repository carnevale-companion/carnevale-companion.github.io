## Dice Roll

Every roll in Carnevale is a pool of D10s scoring Aces, with one Destiny die that decides Criticals and Fumbles. This calculator gives the exact odds of each outcome and the full spread of how many Aces you can expect. See [Dice Rolls](/rules/#dice-rolls) for the rules.
